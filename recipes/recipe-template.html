Bacon, Egg & Cheese Avocado + Chips

PREP TIME

15 minutes

SERVES

1-2

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Russet Potatoes
  • Sour Dough Bread
  • Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Sea Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Eggs

Directions

1

Heat up a pan to medium and melt 2 tablespoons of butter. Crack 2 eggs and cook Sunny-Side-Up.

2

Slice avocado in half, then mash until spreadable. Salt/Pepper &/or lime to desired flavor.

3

Toast your sourdough bread and melt cheese ontop of eggs in pan.

4

Spread mashed avocado onto toast, place eggs ontop of avocado spread

5

Oh yea, fry the fries and ENJOY =)
